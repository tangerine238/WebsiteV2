Voice Controlled Wheelchair

Link

The voice controlled wheelchair aims to give people with more severe mobility issues, greater freedom in their day to day life. Audio input from your phone is sent through a server and deciphered using a vosk model. Commands are recieved from this input and are sent via netcat to the chair. Anything from turning to reversing and stopping is possible. The chair also has a built in emergency stop feature. The chair will automatically stop when too close to an obstacle like a wall.
